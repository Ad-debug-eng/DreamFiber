5 Reasons Your Growing Business Needs a Dedicated Broadband Connection

June 16, 2024 5 min read DreamFiber Team

The Growth Bottleneck: Your Internet Connection

When you first started your business, a basic internet connection was enough. But as you hire more staff, adopt new software, and serve more customers, your network becomes a bottleneck. Upgrading to a dedicated broadband connection is the logical next step.

Here are the top five reasons why your growing business needs to partner with a reliable local ISP like DreamFiber for dedicated internet access.

1. Guaranteed Speeds, Zero Throttling

With residential or standard shared business internet, you suffer from "contention." This means during busy hours (like 9:00 AM or 3:00 PM), your speeds drop because everyone around you is online.

  • A dedicated connection has a 1:1 contention ratio.
  • You are the only user on the line.
  • Your speeds will never be throttled during peak business hours.

2. Symmetrical Upload and Download Speeds

Standard internet is asymmetrical (fast downloads, terribly slow uploads). Modern businesses need fast uploads for:

  • Sending large email attachments.
  • Backing up data to cloud servers.
  • Hosting smooth, lag-free webinars and video meetings.

Dedicated broadband ensures your upload speed matches your download speed perfectly.

3. Enhanced Network Security

Cybersecurity is a massive concern for modern enterprises. Shared broadband lines carry a slightly higher risk of data interception.

  • A dedicated connection provides a private, isolated route for your data.
  • It is much easier to implement advanced firewall configurations and VPNs on a dedicated line.
  • Protecting customer data and internal IP becomes significantly more manageable.

4. Enterprise-Grade Service Level Agreements (SLAs)

What happens when your internet goes down? With standard broadband, you are put in a queue, and it might take days to get a technician.

  • Dedicated broadband comes with a strict SLA.
  • Providers are contractually obligated to fix issues within hours (e.g., a 4-hour or 6-hour fix time).
  • You get priority, localized tech support from companies like DreamFiber, minimizing business disruption.

5. Seamless Scalability

A growing business is unpredictable. You might double your workforce in six months.

  • Dedicated broadband lines are highly scalable.
  • Providers can easily increase your bandwidth remotely without needing to dig up roads or install new hardware in your office.
  • Your internet grows effortlessly alongside your company.

Conclusion

Don't let a slow network limit your company's potential. A dedicated broadband connection offers the speed, security, and support you need to scale confidently. Get in touch with DreamFiber to build a network that supports your ambitions.
