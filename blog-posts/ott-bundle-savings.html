The Ultimate Guide to Bundled Broadband and OTT Subscriptions

June 2, 2017 4 min read DreamFiber Team

The New Era of Home Entertainment

The days of paying separately for cable TV and internet are over. The modern home relies entirely on streaming. We watch movies on Netflix, sports on IPTV, and shows on Prime Video.

However, managing five different subscription bills while paying for high-speed internet gets expensive fast. The solution? Bundled Broadband and OTT Subscriptions. Here is why local ISPs like DreamFiber are shifting to all-in-one entertainment packages.

What is a Broadband + OTT Bundle?

A bundle is a single monthly subscription plan that provides you with high-speed fiber internet and free or heavily discounted access to premium Over-The-Top (OTT) platforms (like Netflix, Disney+, Amazon Prime, and regional streaming apps).

3 Reasons You Should Switch to a Bundled Plan

1. Massive Cost Savings

When you pay for Netflix, Prime, Hotstar, and your internet connection separately, the costs add up to a premium price tag.

  • ISPs negotiate bulk deals with streaming companies.
  • By bundling, you can save anywhere from 20% to 40% on your total monthly entertainment and utility expenses.

2. One Bill, Zero Hassle

Instead of tracking different billing cycles and updating your credit card on five different platforms, a bundle consolidates everything. You receive one single, predictable bill at the end of the month from your ISP.

3. Guaranteed Speeds for 4K Streaming

OTT platforms demand massive amounts of data. If you pay for Premium Netflix to watch shows in 4K Ultra HD, you need an internet connection that can handle it.

  • 4K streaming requires a minimum of 25 Mbps of dedicated bandwidth per screen.
  • Bundled plans are specifically designed with high data allowances and speeds optimized for heavy, buffer-free streaming.

Don't Forget About IPTV

Alongside traditional OTT apps, many bundled plans now include IPTV (Internet Protocol Television).

  • IPTV replaces your old satellite dish by streaming live TV channels directly over your fiber internet connection.
  • It offers interactive features like pause/rewind live TV, video-on-demand, and superior picture quality without weather disruptions.

How to Choose the Right Bundle

When looking at broadband packages, consider your household's viewing habits:

  • Do you need multiple simultaneous streams for different family members? (Opt for a 150 Mbps+ plan).
  • Are you primarily interested in live sports? (Ensure the bundle includes the specific OTT apps that hold broadcasting rights).

Conclusion: Upgrade Your Living Room with DreamFiber
