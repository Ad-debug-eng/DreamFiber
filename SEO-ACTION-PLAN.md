## Step 1 — Create / claim your Google Business Profile (highest priority)

This is 80% of the battle for "near me" and "Dream Cable" searches.

1. Go to **https://business.google.com** → "Manage now" → search "DreamFiber".
   - If it doesn't exist, **create it**.
   - If a listing already exists for your Chakan office, **claim** it.
2. **Business name:** `DreamFiber` (do NOT name it "Dream Cable" — that's the whole
   point of separating from the Akurdi listing).
3. **Primary category:** `Internet service provider`
   **Additional categories:** `Cable company`, `Telecommunications service provider`,
   `Internet marketing service` is NOT relevant — skip it.
4. **Address:** enter your real Chakan office address (more specific than just
   "Chakan" — full street/landmark). This must be a DIFFERENT address from Akurdi.
5. **Service area:** add `Chakan` and `Chakan MIDC`.
6. **Phone:** primary `8788536698`. Add the website `https://dreamservices.in`.
7. **Hours:** set them (the website schema assumes 9:00 AM – 9:00 PM, 7 days — match
   reality and tell me if different so I update the website).
8. **Verification:** Google will verify by postcard / phone / video. Complete it —
   an unverified profile barely ranks.
9. **Fill everything:** description (mention DreamFiber, Chakan, Chakan MIDC, lease
   line, broadband, IPTV, OTT, "since 2006"), services with prices, and **10+ real
   photos** (office, installation team, router/ONT, signboard). Profiles with photos
   and complete info rank far above sparse ones — this is how you beat Akurdi.

## Step 2 — Make your profile clearly DISTINCT from the Akurdi listing

You can't remove your uncle's listing, so make Google see yours as a separate entity:

- **Different name** (DreamFiber vs Dream Cable).
- **Different address** (Chakan vs Akurdi).
- **Different primary phone.**
- If the Akurdi listing is a **duplicate of YOUR business** (same legal entity), you
  can report it in GBP → "Suggest an edit" → "Close or remove" → "Duplicate". Only do
  this if it's genuinely a duplicate of *your* operation, not a separate franchise.
- If customers confuse the two, your steady stream of good reviews (Step 3) will make
  yours the obvious, trusted choice.

## Step 3 — Reviews engine (this is how you out-rank bad reviews)

Volume + recency + rating of reviews is a massive ranking + trust signal. The Akurdi
listing has bad reviews; you beat it by collecting many genuine good ones.

1. In GBP, get your **review short link** (GBP → "Ask for reviews" → copy link).
2. After **every installation or support call**, send this via WhatsApp:

   > Hi! Thanks for choosing **DreamFiber** 🙏 If you're happy with your connection,
   > a quick Google review really helps our local team. It takes 30 seconds:
   > [your review link]

3. Aim for a steady trickle (e.g. 3–5 new reviews/week) rather than a sudden burst.
4. **Reply to every review** (good and bad) — Google rewards active profiles.
5. Never buy fake reviews — Google detects and penalizes this.
